## About this website
I used to blog. Blogger, Wordpress, LiveJournal, Tumblr ... You name it, I was on it. Then I stopped.

In recent times I've been missing it though and so at the back end of 2024, after a lot of soul-searching, research as well as a couple of prematurely aborted attempts, I started this website with intentions of getting back into it. I did it a little bit differently this time around. I didn't want to be at the mercy of whichever corporation owned the platform I published on anymore, so I set the website up in a way that puts everything firmly under my control. This space is mine, a personal playground to mess around in with writing, web development and web design.

Things didn't exactly go according to plan and after February 2025 updates ground to a halt. The website's still here though, and as of June 2026 that is a deliberate choice. I made some changes and posted an update to the [Now-page]({{site.baseurl}}/now) at the start of that month, but that shouldn't be taken as a promise to keep this space more active. Right now it's just a place where I publish some writings, sometimes.
