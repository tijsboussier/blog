## About This Website
I used to blog. Blogger, Wordpress, LiveJournal, Tumblr ... You name it, I was on it. Then I stopped.

I started to miss it soon after, so at the back end of 2024, after a lot of soul-searching, some research as well as a couple of prematurely aborted attempts, I started this website with intentions of getting back into it. Things didn't exactly go according to plan and after February 2025 updates ground to a halt. I updated the [Now-page]({{site.baseurl}}/now) (and now this page, too) in June 2026, but I don't assume too much from this; probably this will continue to be a place that I come back to from time to time, with big breaks in between. That's okay

Not wanting to be at the mercy of whichever corporation owns the platform I publish on anymore, I set the website up in a way that puts everything firmly under my control. This space is mine, a personal playground to mess around in with writing, web development and web design.
